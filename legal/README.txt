Welcome to the VeniceGeo Project!

Like all good open source projects, we need to preserve and protect the
copyrights and attributions of our source code; as a contributor, you have
responsibilities to help us in this regard. Please read the following three
sections regarding our requirements.

If you have any questions or concerns, please email us at venice@radiantblue.com.

--- --- ---

(1) We require that each individual or corporation sign a Contributor License
Agreement (CLA), affirming that any source code they produce will be assigned to
us as copyright holder and will be put under the Apache license.

The agreement for corporations is at:

    https://github.com/venicegeo/venice/blob/master/legal/C-CLA.txt

The agreement for individuals is at:

   https://github.com/venicegeo/venice/blob/master/legal/I-CLA.txt

You can use either the Individual or Corporate agreement, whichever you feel is
most appropriate.

Note that these CLAs are taken directly from the Apache Foundationâ€™s templates;
we have not modified it in any way except for the contact info.

When signed, pls email a scanned copy to venice-cla@radiantblue.com.

--- --- ---

(2) We require that every repository contain our LICENSE.txt in the root
directory.

The file can be found at:

    https://github.com/venicegeo/venice/blob/master/legal/LICENSE.txt

--- --- ---

(3) We require that every source code file contain our license header text at
the top of the file.

The text can be found at:

    https://github.com/venicegeo/venice/blob/master/legal/LICENSE-HEADER.txt
